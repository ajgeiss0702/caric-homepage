Educational Background


I'm an alumnus of the Computer Science Department at the University of Arizona. I graduated with my M.S. in Computer Science in 1996. While at the U of A I worked on the Imager for Mars Pathfinder project (IMP) in the Lunar and Planetary Laboratory. Here's a picture I took of myself with the IMP while it was still in the lab, before its one-way journey to Mars. Note: Pathfinder was the one which made it to the surface and completed its mission! And later was instrumental in saving both Val Kilmer and Matt Damon!

Before attending U of A, I received my B.S. in Computer Science and B.S. in Math from Missouri State University.
